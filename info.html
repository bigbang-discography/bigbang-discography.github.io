ABOUT THIS SITE

Hello! I'm TxMikura, long time Mexican VIP.
I created this website with the intention of serving as a reference for all of BIGBANG's physical releases from Korea and Japan (or as many as I'm aware of, at least). I realized many other sites, including official ones, have a few releases undocumented, so I put myself up for the task. I hope the info provided here is useful to other VIPs and collectors. ♥

What will you find here?

A (hopefully) comprehensive list of all of BIGBANG's (group, sub-unit and solo) physical releases and their different editions/variations. This includes: CDs, Vinyl LPs, DVDs, Blu-rays, Music Cards, Playbuttons, Photobooks, and more. This list DOES NOT include: releases outside of Korea and Japan, digital releases, goods and merchandise.

Where did this info come from?

The info on this site is collected from multiple sources, both official and unofficial, such as: YG Family, YGEX, Universal Music Japan, Wikipedia, Generasia, Discogs, My BIGBANG Collection, and my personal collection.

In addition to the sections still in the works, I have plans of creating a template maker so people can make personalized wish-lists for their collections, stay tuned!
